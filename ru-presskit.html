Pitch

Your brother's unexpected death leads you to unsettling locations in search of floppy disks with cursed retro games.

Description

Reason Unfound is a short first-person horror game where you hunt for cursed floppy disks containing retro games, reminiscent of Creepypasta.

Discover the macabre world of cursed retro games stored on each floppy. As you delve into these unsettling experiences, you'll get closer to understanding the circumstances surrounding your brother's death. The answers may not be straightforward, but each completed game will bring you one step closer to the elusive truth.

Features

  • A short horror experience
  • First-person combat
  • Atmospheric and eerie locations
  • Retro aesthetic
  • 2D and 3D gameplay

About Tissue Inu & Daisy Games

Reason Unfound is being developed by Tissue Inu and Daisy Games. Tissue Inu, an indie game studio founded by Milo and Giulia, previously developed and published Her Name Was Fire. Daisy Games, a studio created by solo developer Martin, previously developed and published puzzle games such as Crossroad OS, Sokobos and Dark Sheep.
